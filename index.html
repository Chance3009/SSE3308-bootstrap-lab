Dining and Cafeteria

The meeting of cultures and races at UPM has served to enrich the institution with a large assortment of delicious food. There are more than 10 cafeterias scattered all over the UPM compound, offering a variety of food choices in an array of flavours at reasonable prices. Students and staff can choose to indulge in the unique range of delicacies, which include kampung, Western and Middle Eastern, during their stay at UPM.

Expo Hill

After a long-day of work and study, visit Expo Hill , our recreational and physical activity location, Expo Hill or Bukit Ekspo as it is popularly known, is where you can release stress by exercising or simply enjoying the beauty of nature. This huge area of green is located in front of the Office of the Director of University Agricultural Park (TPU), UPM. Pack a picnic hamper and head for Expo Hill with the family; organize a faculty function or some other corporate event; have a great Family Day, an outdoorexhibition, a photo shoot or even try your hand at being a fisherman, maybe experience a kayaking afternoon. Expo Hill is an all-purpose facility that offers immense possibilities for activities on land and on water.
